✨ Core Capabilities

Leveraging deep learning and vast data to transform groundwater management from reactive to predictive.

Multilingual Chatbot

Access complex groundwater data in *plain language* and native dialects—Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and more—making it truly accessible.

Real-time Analytics

Visualize live groundwater levels, map contamination, and see drought forecasts instantly, powered by *INGRES* data integration.

Water management Planner

Get *AI-driven, crop-specific* irrigation recommendations. Minimize water waste, optimize usage, and significantly boost crop yield.

CNN-LSTM Prediction Engine

Our hybrid deep learning model predicts *groundwater table trends* across short, medium, and long-term time horizons with high fidelity.

Critical Water Stress Alerts

Proactive identification of critical water stress and drought zones, allowing for *timely, life-saving interventions* by authorities and farmers.

Source-Verified Transparency

Every response is *source-verified* and traceable back to the raw INGRES records, building trust and academic rigor.

❓ Quick Answers (FAQs)

Can ZENFLOW work in rural areas with low internet?

Yes! ZENFLOW is highly optimized for *low-bandwidth environments* and can operate efficiently on basic mobile networks. We are actively developing a robust offline support feature for data access.

Is the chatbot available in regional Indian languages?

Absolutely. ZENFLOW's core value is accessibility. We support *Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and several other regional languages* thanks to our advanced multilingual NLP engine.

How accurate are the irrigation predictions?

Predictions are based on a triangulated approach: real-time *INGRES data*, validated crop models, and up-to-the-minute weather forecasts. Accuracy is continuously validated and improves with localized user data inputs.

Is ZENFLOW part of the Smart India Hackathon 2025?

*Yes!* ZENFLOW is proudly developed as a problem statement solution for SIH2025, focused on revolutionizing groundwater data accessibility and management across India.
