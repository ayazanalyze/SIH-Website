Can ZENFLOW work in rural areas with low internet?

Yes! ZENFLOW is highly optimized for *low-bandwidth environments* and can operate efficiently on basic mobile networks. We are actively developing a robust offline support feature for data access.

Is the chatbot available in regional Indian languages?

Absolutely. ZENFLOW's core value is accessibility. We support *Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and several other regional languages* thanks to our advanced multilingual NLP engine.

How accurate are the irrigation predictions?

Predictions are based on a triangulated approach: real-time *INGRES data*, validated crop models, and up-to-the-minute weather forecasts. Accuracy is continuously validated and improves with localized user data inputs.

Is ZENFLOW part of the Smart India Hackathon 2025?

*Yes!* ZENFLOW is proudly developed as a problem statement solution for SIH2025, focused on revolutionizing groundwater data accessibility and management across India.
